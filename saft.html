How vesting works Tokens are locked in the SWRSaft smart contract. After the mainnet launch event (triggered by the SWR team), a 6-month cliff begins. After the cliff, tokens unlock linearly over 12 months — meaning 1/12th of your post-cliff allocation unlocks each month. You can claim at any time; unclaimed tokens continue to accumulate.
