What We Do

Real Assets. Digital Access.

SWR is a regulated blockchain platform that converts Saudi Arabia's largest real-world assets into tradeable digital tokens — with full Shariah compliance and CMA authorization.

🏗️

Tokenize

Asset sponsors upload legal documents, define token structure, and issue fractional tokens through our CMA-compliant issuance portal. Each token represents legal fractional ownership, protected in a Saudi SPV.

💼

Invest

Verified investors buy asset tokens from SAR 100. Full KYC and Shariah compliance enforced on-chain. Portfolio dashboard shows real-time valuations and accrued yield across all holdings.

🔄

Trade & Earn

Trade tokens 24/7 on SWR's compliant secondary market with T+0 settlement. Yield — rent, sukuk coupons, dividends — distributed automatically in SARX to your wallet. Claim anytime, no expiry.

Process

How SWR Works

Four steps from asset selection to on-chain yield — transparent, regulated, and Shariah-compliant at every stage.

01

Verify Your Identity

Complete KYC/AML in under 10 minutes. Identity certified on-chain via the KYCWhitelist contract — you can hold and trade regulated tokens immediately after approval.

02

Browse Assets

Explore tokenized assets — each with legal documentation, Shariah certificate, yield history, and SPV details. Filter by asset class, yield, or risk profile.

03

Invest from SAR 100

Buy asset tokens using SARX (SAR stablecoin) directly in your wallet. Transactions settle in seconds on-chain. Full ownership record from day one.

04

Earn & Trade

Yield accumulates automatically in SARX. Claim anytime — no expiry, no minimum. Trade on the secondary market 24/7 with full compliance enforcement on every transfer.

Technical Foundation

Built for Institutional Trust

Every design decision prioritizes regulatory compliance, investor protection, and code transparency.

🔐

Security Audited

Internal audit completed. All critical vulnerabilities fixed — O(1) yield accumulator, SafeERC20, Math.mulDiv for limit checks. Third-party audit in progress.

⏱️

48h Timelock

All admin actions (role changes, distributor updates) queue through a 48-hour timelock. Investors have time to react before any change takes effect.

🔑

Multisig Admin

Mainnet will use a Gnosis Safe 3-of-5 multisig for all admin actions. No single key can control the platform. Compliance officer is separate from admin.

📖

Open Source

All smart contracts are public on GitHub and verified on Etherscan. Anyone can read, audit, and verify exactly what executes on-chain.
